CHAPLAINCY DEPARTMENT

The chaplain department of Save Souls Afrika plays a vital role in providing emotional and spiritual support to individuals in need.

Here are some key works the department focuses on:

Key Responsibilities

  • Providing emotional and spiritual support: We offer counselling, prayer and guidance to individuals facing challenges or crises.
  • Training and equipping volunteers: Chaplains recruit and train volunteers to provide spiritual care and support to those in need.
  • Crisis intervention and response: The department provides critical support during times of crisis, offering a caring presence and spiritual guidance.

Potential Areas of Focus

  • Hospital and health care Chaplaincy: Providing spiritual care to patients, families and health care staff.
  • Community chaplaincy: Offering spiritual support and guidance to individuals in community settings.
  • Disaster response and recovery: Offering spiritual support and guidance to individuals during times of disasters or crises.

Social Benefits for Youth

  • Emotional Support: Confidential counselling chaplains provide a safe and confidential space for youth to share their concerns and emotions.
  • Mental Health Support: Chaplains offer emotional support and guidance to help manage stress, anxiety, trauma and other mental health challenges.
  • Community Building: Chaplains create a sense of belonging and community among youth.
  • Peer Support: The chaplaincy department facilitates building relationships and supporting one another.
  • Above All: The chaplaincy department of Save Souls Afrika focuses on making witnesses and proclaiming to all people the everlasting gospel of the Three Angel’s Messages in preparation for His soon return (Jesus Christ).
