CHARITY DEPARTMENT OF SAVE SOULS AFRIKA

The charity department of Save Souls Afrika is focusing on doing the following works to impact social well-being of the community:
  • Empowering orphaned children through education: Providing scholastic materials and sponsorship.
  • Business training and mentorship: Training and mentorship programmes to help entrepreneurs develop business skills and partnerships.
  • Entrepreneurship programmes: Supporting start-up and early stage businesses.
  • Vocational training: Training in skills such as carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, and tailoring.
  • Cooperatives: Supporting cooperatives that allow community members to pool resources and share benefits.
  • Economic empowerment for women: Programmes that specifically target economic empowerment for women, such as business training.
