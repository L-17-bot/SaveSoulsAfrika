Welcome To Save Souls Afrika

Save Souls Afrika is a non profit organisation that deals in charity and chaplaincy works.
We offer community chaplaincy and counseling, creating a community that recognises God.
The charity department of Save Souls Afrika is not only based on charity works (giving to the poor) but we also do economic empowerment programmes to empower people and fight poverty and economic crisis.
"We bring hope, healing and transformation to individuals and communities in Africa, through Chaplaincy and Charity work, demonstrating God's love and Compassion."
